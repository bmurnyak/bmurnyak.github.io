Glioma Methylation Pipeline

Cancer Epigenomics · Python · TCGA · Survival Analysis

Companion analysis to manuscript under review on PARP/CD274 co-regulation in glioma. Genome-wide methylation analysis integrating TCGA patient data, gene expression, and clinical outcomes.

29,747 significant DMRs (FDR<0.05) identified
CD274 methylation correlates with PARP1/2/3 expression (all p<0.05)
Kaplan-Meier survival: log-rank p = 1.33×10⁻⁸⁸ (n=915)
PARP2, PARG, CD274 predict LGG survival (all p<0.01)
